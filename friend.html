Friends

My Muder

I truly appreciate my circle of friends, "Muder," who I treasure more than words can express. We first met in 2022 when we were rookies in JPK, and from the very beginning, we faced countless challenges together. Despite the tough times, we stuck by each other, learning, growing, and overcoming obstacles as a team. Our friendship has only deepened through these shared experiences, and now, I can't imagine my life without them. They are not just my friends; they’ve become family. I love them so much that I’ve come to declare them my favorite "hello" and my hardest "goodbye." Through every laugh, every struggle, and every moment we’ve shared, I am forever grateful for them and the bond we’ve built.

My Best Teh Ais

Alis and Anisah are my best friends, my partners in crime, my ride-or-die or whatever you want to call it, they’re my people. We’ve been through it all since day one of diploma, and I can’t imagine my journey without them. From witnessing my breakdowns over assignments to being there through every struggle, they’ve supported me in ways I could never repay. They were also the first friends I had sleepovers with, and we’ve created countless memories together. Too many stories, too many laughs, and too many moments that I will forever cherish. They’re more than friends to me, they’re family, and I’m so grateful to have them by my side every step of the way.
